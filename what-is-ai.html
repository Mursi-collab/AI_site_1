What Is Artificial Intelligence?

Home | AI Around Me | How AI Helps Me | Challenges & Ethics | Reflection

Artificial Intelligence is technology that can think, learn, and make decisions similar to humans but with far more data and speed. From your phone to your favorite apps, AI works behind the scenes to make life easier and more connected.

AI matters today because it's reshaping how we communicate, learn, work, and play. It's not just about robots; it's about tools that adapt to your needs in real time.
